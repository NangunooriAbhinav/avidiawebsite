Frequently Asked Questions

What is penetration testing?

Penetration testing is a security testing method that involves simulating an attack on a system or network to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses that could be exploited by malicious actors. Its goal is to help organizations improve their security posture by providing recommendations for remediation.

Does my organization require a pentest?

Penetration testing is extremely important for organizations that handle sensitive data or rely on computer systems and networks for their operations. It helps to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses that could be exploited by attackers, and provides a roadmap for improving security defenses. Without regular penetration testing, organizations risk being compromised by cyber attacks and facing financial losses, reputation damage, and legal liabilities.

Do you make our applications hackproof?

No, we do not make an application entirely hackproof. Even after a successful penetration test, new vulnerabilities can be discovered or new attack methods can be developed. Therefore, it is important to view penetration testing as one component of a comprehensive security program. 

However, we will help you  lower the chances of getting attacked by a significant percentage.
